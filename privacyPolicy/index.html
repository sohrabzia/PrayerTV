Privacy

Effortless. Private. Open.

At PrayerTV, we believe privacy isn't just a feature, it's the foundation of everything we build. We designed this experience to be as transparent as it is spiritual, ensuring your focus remains on your devotion, not your data.

Intentional Simplicity

We do not collect what we do not need. PrayerTV requires no accounts, tracks no identities, and stores no personal history. Your interactions with the divine are yours alone.
